The Trouble with Bears

By Evan Wild

Bears are proud, independent creatures… Nothing like the ones on TV.

We explore their true nature and challenge the media portrayal.

Types of bear

Bears come in large and medium sizes. No small species exist except cubs.

Urban vs. wild bears have drastically different lifestyles.

Facts about wild vs. urban bears
Bear type Coat Adult size Habitat Lifespan Diet
Wild Brown or black 1.4–2.8 m Woods and forests 25–28 years Fish, meat, plants
Urban North Face jacket (metaphorical) 1.4–2.2 m Alleys and suburbs 20–32 years Trash, donuts, sushi
